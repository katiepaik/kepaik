KATIE PAIK
ABOUT
Topography of Typography — 2019

A series of eight poster-pamphlets inspired by El Lissitzky's "Topography of Typography". An experiment with typography as expressed over multiple iterations within a single framework. Digital .gif & printed on risograph, 11"x17".


Birdwatcher — 2020

This collection looks at an unorthodox piece of the metropolitan landscape—the wandering pigeons of Port Authority, and analyzes their bizarre presence as documented through social media. All data observed, collected and visualized by self.Printed as part of a class series, A4 pages.


Goose (Brand Design) — 2019

A fictional travel brand ideated and executed to fill a market gap for Gen X. Applications include desktop site and mobile app, printable itinerary, postcards, luggage accessories, etc. Digital presentation and printed brand book available.


Commuter Calendar — 2019

A 30 day calendar for commuters to creatively track bus conditions, an homage the NJ Transit monthly bus pass. Incorporates guilloche patterns in the visual design. Tearable booklet of cards, 3.5"x2.5".


Virtual Teller — 2020

Virtual Teller product interface. Product ideated for Cornell Tech BigCo Studio in partnership with Verizon to leverage 5G in the fintech industry. Wireframe in AdobeXD.


Weather API — 2019

Interactive canvas based on the live humidity and wind speed statistics in 10 different cities. Built using OpenWeatherMap API. Code for web, HTML-CSS-Javascript.


ARIVA (Motion, AE) — 2019

Logo sequence done for loading page of a personalized navigation app. Participated in the ideation and UI/UX design of ARIVA in collaboration with a team at Cornell Tech Product Studio. Animation. ARIVA wireframe and prototype demo available upon request.


Council of Korean Americans (Branding) — 2019

Conceptual campaign rebranding for the Council of Korean Americans. Outputs include a complete digital and print brand guide, wordmark, website re-design, stickers, and wearables. Booklet print and digital renderings done for each deliverable.


Plant Parenthood (Interaction Design, Figma) — 2019

Branding and interaction design for an application that personalizes a plant and educates users on household botanicals. Figma wireframe prototype.
Rockwell Type Specimen — 2018

Card-style type specimen with a graphic poster wrapper. Displayed as individual cards or a poster-style puzzle. 7"x7" bristol cards, forming a 3 by 3 square matrix.


Chalaza (Publication) — 2018

The chalaza (noun) is composed of two twisted membranous strips which join the yolk to the ends of the shell. A publication of curated texts and image dedicated to the egg and the greater implications of its form. Printed book, 72 pages, 5"x8".


Poster Calendar — 2018

Foldable event calendar design for the Boryeong Mud Festival in South Korea. Side 1 displays a general outline / Side 2 presents a detailed account of events with corresponding descriptions. Double-sided plotter print, 24"x36".


Reimagining the Alphabet (Experimental) — 2018

A reordering and visual mapping of the alphabet based on typographic characteristics of the typeface Gill Sans. Digital and print, 11"x17".
